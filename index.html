ABOUT
ME

I'm a Mechanical Engineering student at Cal Poly SLO, graduating December 2026 with a focus on composite structures and advanced manufacturing. I work at the intersection of analysis and hands-on fabrication.

With Cal Poly Racing's Baja SAE team, I've designed and built vacuum-infused Carbon-Kevlar skid plates, machined aluminum steering wheel inserts to tight tolerances, and fabricated safety components that helped the team pass technical inspection first at the 2025 competition.

My composites testing work using Toray TC250 pre-preg matched analytical micromechanics predictions within 2% — and my SAMPE I-beam hit 9kN at just 312g. I build things that perform.

PROJECTS

01
Composite Skid Plate
Fabricated an impact-resistant three-piece vehicle skid plate for off-road racing. Carbon-Kevlar skid plate with Rohacell core manufactured using vacuum infusion — 43% lighter than aluminum equivalent. Achieved 6th overall at Baja SAE Arizona 2025 with no significant structural damage.
Vacuum Infusion Carbon-Kevlar Rohacell Core Baja SAE

02
SAMPE Composite I-Beam
Designed and manufactured the lightest composite I-beam to achieve the highest point load in three-point bending. Unidirectional pre-preg carbon fiber tailored for peak stress direction, with Nomex sandwich core. Achieved 9kN load at just 312g.
Pre-preg Carbon Nomex Core Three-Point Bend SAMPE
↗
03
CF Miata Radiator Cowl
Designing and fabricating a lightweight carbon fiber cowl cover to direct increased airflow through the radiator of an NA Mazda Miata. Carbon-fiber pre-preg plate cured to final dimensions with holes and cutouts created via water jet. Currently in progress.
Carbon Fiber Pre-preg Layup Water Jet In Progress
↗
04
Steering Wheel Inserts
Manufactured six aluminum inserts for a composite steering wheel using a manual lathe. Parts turned and faced to within 0.005" of drawing dimensions. Steering wheel was mounted and used in the 2025 Baja SAE competition with no damage to the composite structure.
Manual Lathe Aluminum Machining GD&T Baja SAE
↗
05
ASTM D3039/D3518 Testing
Fabricated and tested unidirectional composite samples using Toray TC250 infused pre-preg to determine mean strength properties per ASTM standards. Samples tested in Instron — experimental results matched analytical micromechanics predictions within 2% error.
ASTM D3039 ASTM D3518 Toray TC250 Instron Testing
↗
06
Fire Ext. Tabs & Headrest
Designed and fabricated safety components to meet SAE competition requirements. Aluminum backplate for SFI foam upholstery cut via water jet; tabs water jet from 0.125" 4340 chromoly steel and welded to chassis. Cal Poly Racing was first through technical inspection at 2025 Baja SAE.
