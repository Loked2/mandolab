Fire Ext. Tabs & Headrest

Safety-critical hardware for the Baja car — headrest and fire-extinguisher mounting tabs waterjet from chromoly and aluminum.

Overview

Tech inspection scrutinizes driver-safety hardware. I made the headrest and fire-extinguisher mounting tabs, with backplates and brackets waterjet from 4340 chromoly and aluminum, then riveted and welded to the chassis.

Cal Poly Racing was first through technical inspection at Baja SAE Arizona 2025 with no safety concerns.

Highlights

  • 4340 chromoly / aluminum, waterjet cut
