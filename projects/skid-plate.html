Composite Skid Plate

An impact-resistant three-piece skid plate manufactured by vacuum infusion, Carbon-Kevlar skins over a Rohacell core, 43% lighter than the aluminum part it replaced.

ApplicationBaja SAE underbody
SkinsCarbon-Kevlar hybrid
CoreRohacell foam
ProcessVacuum infusion
Weight−43% vs aluminum
Result6th overall · AZ 2025
Overview

The skid plate shields the underside of the car from rocks and impacts. I built it as a three-piece Carbon-Kevlar sandwich, the Kevlar adds impact toughness and damage tolerance while the carbon provides stiffness, over a lightweight Rohacell core.

I manufactured the parts using vacuum infusion, which yields a high, consistent fiber-volume fraction and a clean single-shot cure. The finished assembly came out 43% lighter than the aluminum equivalent and survived the full event with no structural damage, contributing to a 6th overall finish at Baja SAE Arizona 2025.

Highlights
  • Carbon-Kevlar hybrid skins for impact tolerance
  • Rohacell-cored sandwich, vacuum infused
  • 43% mass reduction versus aluminum
  • Zero structural damage across competition
